Why Many Small Businesses Are Rejected

Many applicants do not fail because their business is bad. They fail because the lender cannot clearly see proof, structure, and repayment ability.

  • No proper sales records
  • Weak or confusing bank statement
  • No clear business profile
  • No professional loan request letter
  • No realistic repayment plan
